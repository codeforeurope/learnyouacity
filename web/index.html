'Learn you a city' helps you locate streets in a city. You will be presented with a challenge: click the part of the city that you think contains the street of which the name is shown. If you're correct (or close enough), the map will zoom in on the selected quadrant, and you'll be asked to locate the street again. This will continue until either the street is sufficiently located or you've chosen a quadrant not containing the challenge. In either case, the challenge street will be highlighted and clicking the map will zoom out and provide you with a new challenge.
